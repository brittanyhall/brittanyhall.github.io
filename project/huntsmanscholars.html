Huntsman Scholar Program

The Huntsman Scholar Program is a prestigious program at USU where students are hand-picked by the board to participate in various opportunities and become part of a business family. In the HSP, we are expecting to maintain a 3.5 GPA, go on a short study abroad, get excel certified, and network with everyone we can. It has taught me a lot, as well as give me close friends, and I cannot wait for what the future holds.

While being in the scholar program, I have been able to serve as a team lead, a cohort lead, and now the Vice President over Team Leads. This has given me the experience to be over small groups, as well as large groups. It has been a great learning experience and has taught me how to better communicate and present.
